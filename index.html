raigad

Raigad is one of the districts in Konkan Division of Maharashtra and Arabian Sea coast forms western boundary of the district. Most hilly places of the District are in the Sahyadri mountain range. Previously known as Kolaba, it was rechristened as Raigad District.

Torna

Torna Fort, also known as Prachandagad, is a large fort located in Pune district, in the Indian state of Maharashtra.
  

shivnari

Shivneri Fort is an ancient military fortification located near Junnar in Pune district in Maharashtra, India. It is the birthplace of Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of Maratha Empire.

Harihar Fort Nashik distance 190 kilometers it takes five hours to complete this journey. Harihar fort height in feet is 3676 feet above sea level.

Pratapgad

Pratapgad is a mountain fort located in Satara district, in the Western Indian state of Maharashtra. The fort is situated 24 kilometres from the Mahabaleshwar hill station. The fort is now a popular tourist destination
